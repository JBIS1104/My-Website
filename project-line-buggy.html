Contributions

What I built.

This was a team project. Here’s what I personally delivered.

🛠️

Hardware Build

Physically assembled the buggy chassis, drivetrain, and sensor mounts. Ensured structural rigidity and component alignment.

💻

SolidWorks CAD

Produced full parametric 3D models of chassis components and subassemblies. Designed for manufacturability and correct tolerancing.

📈

FEA Validation

Ran Finite Element Analysis on critical structural members. Iterated geometry to bring peak stress below material limits with acceptable safety factors.

⚡

Wiring & Soldering

Designed and executed all electrical connections: motor drivers, IR sensors, power distribution, and microcontroller interface. Tight, reliable harness.

🖼️

PID Coding

Implemented a PID control loop in C/C++ for line-following behaviour. Tuned gains experimentally for reliable tracking across straight sections and curves.

📄

Project Report

Authored the complete mechanical section of the final report, covering design rationale, material selection, FEA methodology, and validation outcomes.
